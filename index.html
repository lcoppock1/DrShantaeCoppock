About Me

As a recent graduate of Walden University, I am an expert in Public Policy and Administration with a specialization in Criminal Justice. Additionally, I am grounded in other knowledge like Philosophy, Human Services, Business Administration, and Humanities.

With over 8 years of experience managing supervisors, officers, and students, I have demonstrated strong leadership across court operations and higher education. My work involves influencing positive behavioral change, such as instituting an organizational wellness unit, and fostering collaboration between judicial stakeholders and law enforcement agencies to achieve shared goals.

Combined with extensive research and publication, I have the experience of working in the industry as a supervisor and training officer in law enforcement working within the City and County of Philadelphia jurisdictions. I am passionate about applying ethics with evidence-based research to develop solutions for complex problems in public safety.
